# Project Overview

## Product Name

ReelMind AI

## Tagline

From Idea to Viral Reel in Minutes

## Product Concept

ReelMind AI is an AI tool for creators that helps turn rough ideas into ready-to-produce short-form video assets. It focuses on the creative workflow behind viral reels: ideas, hooks, scripts, captions, calls to action, thumbnails, voiceover prompts, and video prompts.

## Target Users

- Content creators
- Instagram reel creators
- YouTubers
- Freelancers
- Agencies
- Small businesses

## Core Problem

Creators often struggle with consistency, ideation speed, and content packaging. Even when they have a topic, they still need to shape it into a strong hook, concise script, engaging caption, clear CTA, and visual direction. This creates friction, delays, and creative fatigue.

## Core Solution

ReelMind AI gives creators a guided AI workspace for generating short-form video content assets quickly. Instead of starting from a blank page, users can enter a niche, goal, audience, or rough idea and receive polished creative outputs designed for reels and short-form platforms.

## MVP Goal

The Phase 1 MVP should help a creator generate, save, and revisit a complete reel content package:

- Viral hook
- Reel script
- Caption
- CTA
- Saved project
- Prompt history

The MVP should prove that creators can move from idea to usable content faster than their current manual workflow.

## Product Positioning

ReelMind AI should feel like a premium creative co-pilot for short-form creators. The product should prioritize speed, clarity, aesthetic polish, and repeatable output quality.

## Success Signals

- Users generate multiple content assets in one session.
- Users save projects for later use.
- Users return to previous prompt history.
- Users copy outputs into their creation workflow.
- Users report reduced time from idea to publish-ready concept.
